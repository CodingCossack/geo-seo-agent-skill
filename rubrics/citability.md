# Citability rubric

Score out of 100.

- directness of answer — 30
- self-containment — 25
- structure and readability — 20
- specificity and evidence density — 15
- uniqueness or first-hand value — 10

## Strong passage signs

- answers the likely question in the first lines
- low pronoun ambiguity
- concrete nouns, numbers, dates, examples, or named entities
- compact enough to quote cleanly

## Weak passage signs

- setup before the answer
- vague claims with no evidence
- heavy dependency on surrounding context
- filler, throat-clearing, or repeated abstractions
