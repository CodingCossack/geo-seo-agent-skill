# Technical rubric

Score out of 100.

- renderability and bot-visible HTML — 30
- crawlability and indexability — 20
- canonicalization and metadata — 15
- security and trust headers — 10
- performance-risk indicators — 10
- mobile and URL hygiene — 10
- other structural checks — 5

A page with inaccessible or JS-hidden primary content cannot score well even if
other checks pass.
