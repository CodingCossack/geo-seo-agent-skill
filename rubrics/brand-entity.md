# Brand and entity rubric

Use conservatively.

Score out of 100.

- core third-party profiles exist and are coherent — 35
- entity references or records (for example Wikidata) where relevant — 25
- credible review / discussion presence in the market — 20
- consistency with on-site schema and branding — 20

This rubric is directional. It should not dominate the audit unless the user’s
problem is clearly entity recognition.
