# Content quality and trust worker

Use this worker when the site’s problem is not merely access but whether the
content deserves to be cited.

## Evaluate

- visible author or organization accountability
- expertise signals and first-hand knowledge where relevant
- coverage depth and specificity
- examples, data, methodology, or original insight
- freshness signals where the topic changes over time
- information density vs filler

## Important

Do not repeat unsupported claims about guideline changes or platform behavior.
Keep findings tied to the content in front of you.

Use `rubrics/content.md` for the scoring frame.
